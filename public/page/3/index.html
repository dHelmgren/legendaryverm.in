About Me

"My name is Devin Helmgren. I’m a game designer, programmer, teacher and writer living in the Seattle area. I’ve worked most of my career on games of some form or another, be it for audiences of hundreds of thousands or for an audience of just myself. I’m always looking for ways to expand my sense of what it means for games to be games, and what it means for games to be art."
